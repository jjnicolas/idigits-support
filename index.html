Math Puzzle Challenge

How to Play

Combine six numbers using +, −, ×, and ÷ to reach the target number. Select a number, choose an operation, then select another number. Chain operations to hit the goal in as few moves as possible.

Tips

  • Each game has 5 stages with increasing difficulty.
  • Earn up to 3 stars per stage — 3 stars means you matched the target exactly.
  • Use the undo button to try different approaches.
  • Switch between Easy and Hard mode in Settings → iDigits.
  • Build a daily streak to earn shields, which let you peek at solutions without losing points.
